Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

Cookie is a self-hosted recipe management application. This privacy policy applies when Cookie is operated in public mode with user accounts enabled.

What data we collect

  • Username — chosen by you during registration, used to identify your account.
  • Password — stored as a secure, one-way hash (PBKDF2-SHA256). We never store your password in plain text.
  • Profile preferences — display name, theme, unit preference, avatar colour.
  • Recipe data — recipes you import, favourites, collections, cooking history, and AI-generated content (tips, remixes).
  • Session data — a functional cookie to keep you logged in (expires after 12 hours of inactivity).

What we do NOT collect

  • Email addresses — your email is used once during registration to send a verification link, then immediately discarded. It is never stored in our database, log files, or anywhere on disk.
  • Tracking or analytics data — no page views, click tracking, or user behaviour analytics.
  • Advertising data — no advertising identifiers or profiles.
  • Location data — we do not track your location.
  • Device fingerprints — we do not fingerprint your browser or device.

How we use your data

Your data is used solely to provide the recipe management service: storing your recipes, preferences, and cooking history. We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third party.

Legal basis for processing

Under UK GDPR (UK Data Protection Act 2018), we process your data on the basis of:

  • Consent — you consent to data processing by creating an account and accepting this privacy policy.
  • Legitimate interest — processing necessary to provide the service you have requested.

Cookies

We use only functional cookies required for the service to work:

  • Session cookie — keeps you logged in. Expires after 12 hours. HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Lax.
  • CSRF cookie — protects against cross-site request forgery attacks.

We do not use any tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party cookies.

Data retention

  • Your account data is retained for as long as your account exists.
  • Unverified accounts are automatically deleted after 24 hours.
  • When you delete your account, all your data is permanently and irreversibly removed.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — view your data via the application interface.
  • Rectification — update your profile and preferences at any time.
  • Erasure — delete your account and all associated data from Settings.
  • Data portability — your recipe data is available through the application.
  • Object — contact the site administrator if you have concerns.

Data security

  • All connections are encrypted via HTTPS.
  • Passwords are hashed with PBKDF2-SHA256 (industry standard).
  • Rate limiting protects against brute-force attacks.
  • The database is backed up daily with encryption at rest.

Contact

For data protection concerns, contact the site administrator. If you are unsatisfied with the response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).
